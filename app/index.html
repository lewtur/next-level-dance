ABOUT US

Founded in 2013, Next Level Dance is dedicated to providing quality dance training for all ages and abilities. Through our passion and love of dance, we aim to inspire and teach each student to strive to their full potential.

When we first opened our doors we were running our classes from a local church hall, as the studio began to grow we then progressed onto running from our local college. Over the next few years the student enrolment continued to grow which meant we were more than ready to move into our very own premises. Today we have an amazing 3 studio state of the art dance school right in the heart of Burnley town centre.

Next Level Dance offers various dance class options and price ranges which allows students to decide whether they want to dance recreationally or take it to the next level.

Join the school that has competed locally and nationally to great success. Take part in yearly shows and appearances within the community.
